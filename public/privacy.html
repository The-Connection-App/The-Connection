Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 1, 2025

Our Commitment

We value your trust above all. We will never sell, rent, or lease your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

  • Email address (for login and account management).
  • User-generated content you post (communities, events, posts).

How We Use Information

  • To provide login and authentication.
  • To display posts, communities, and events you create or join.
  • For moderation and safety (report/block features).

Sharing of Information

We only share your data with trusted service providers (e.g., email or hosting services) strictly to deliver functionality. They are contractually prohibited from using your data for their own purposes.

Your Rights

  • You may delete your account in-app at any time (Settings → Delete Account).
  • Upon deletion, your personal data and posted content will be soft-deleted or removed from public view.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: contact@theconnection.app or to report abuse: abuse@theconnection.app
